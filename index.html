Frequently Asked Questions

What date should I choose for coverage? +

You can choose any start date from today up to 90 days in the future. Coverage cannot be backdated.

Why is my profession required? +

Different industries have different risk profiles. This helps us tailor the general liability and property coverage specifically for your business activities.

What if I don't see my industry? +

If your specific industry isn't in the auto-suggestions, try typing a broader category (e.g. "Retail" instead of a specific item). If you still can't find it, please call us to complete your quote.

Why do you need my Date of Birth & Gender? +

This information is used strictly to verify identity and assess eligibility under standard commercial underwriting criteria in Canada.
